Service

FORMAT operates in two distinct roles. As a Production Practice studio, it provides a comprehensive range of spatial solutions, addressing specific design challenges through conceptualizing, designing, and implementing solutions to optimize both functionality and aesthetics for clients' needs.

Additionally, FORMAT serves as a brand developing unit products produced in limited quantities. This approach enhances personalization for its audience, offering distinctive, high-quality pieces tailored for individual spaces.

Product

FORMAT focuses on developing products using a modular system to address space-related issues, offering significant potential for future development. This concept allows for various activations and flexibilities to be implemented effectively.

Additionally, FORMAT produces limited-quantity unit products, leveraging advanced tools, human resources, and robust manufacturing processes. This approach aims to create products that integrate carefully curated programs and a distinctive design concept characterized by strong aesthetics.

Material

FORMAT uses high-quality metal known for its durability and lasting quality in producing its products. This material ensures that FORMAT's offerings are strong and resilient, capable of enduring daily use effectively.

The use of metal results in products with clean lines and timeless designs, making them versatile options for various needs. This aesthetic appeal, combined with metal's durability, guarantees that FORMAT's offerings maintain their relevance and functionality across different settings and applications, standing the test of time.
